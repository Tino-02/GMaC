Frequently Asked Questions

What is included in the trip cost?

The trip cost includes accommodations, meals, ground transportation, project materials, and guided cultural experiences.

How many trips are organized each year?

We organize two to three trips annually, each providing unique opportunities to serve and explore Ghana.

Do I need any special skills to volunteer?

No special skills are required. We welcome volunteers from all backgrounds and provide necessary training on-site.
